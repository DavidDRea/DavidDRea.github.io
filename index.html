HTML5: the structural building block of the web. I am proficient in HTML5 and enjoy building solid, semantic pages, accessible to all.

HTML5 is great, but without CSS3 the web would be a dull place. Creating beautiful, responsive sites is where the fun really starts for me.

Javascript is by far the most exciting part for me, and the language I enjoy working with the most. From front end to back end, anything is possible.
